What is Streak?

With the people using Duolingo everyday, Duolingo motivates the user to use Duolingo everyday with the help of streaks.Streak shows how the people are using Duolingo on daily basis. If the person forgets about the lesson then duolingo also reminds them about it with different quotes. According to the data given by duolingo, more than one million people have their 365 days streak on duolingo.

Streak Society

As mentioned above, one million people registered in the duolingo among 500 million users have already got their 1 year streak on duolingo. After a year streak they are welcomed to a streak society which basically motivates them to pursue their language goals even further.

Streak Society

Streak Freeze in the duolingo helps to freeze the day when the user on't do the lesson. For the streak freeze the user will already get one while completing few lessons but for more of them they have to spend gems which is considered as money in the Duolingo to purchase the additional features for user.
