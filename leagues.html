Leagues in Duolingo

With the lesson learned, the users get certain XPs from which they can compete in the league. There are ten leagues in Duolingo : Bronze,Silver, Gold, Sapphire, Ruby, Emerald, Amethyst, Pearl, Obsidian, Diamond. These leagues helps to keep up the competitive spirit to learn the languages often on a day.
